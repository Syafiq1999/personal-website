We promote the basic design concepts:
1. Contrast
Size, color, weight, opacity, etc
Creates hierachy of importance
2. Repetition
Repeat similar elements (colors, shapes, size, etc) to make the design cohesive and balanced
3. Alignment
All elements should have a locational relationship to each other (lined-up)
Direct the flow and direction of the user's focus
4. Proximity
Group similar components for clearer definition
"Blocks" of ideas make a more interesting layout and will more likely hold the users attention
